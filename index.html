ABOUT US

The Menses Friend is a revolutionary system designed for safe and eco-friendly menstrual waste disposal. Our machine features a special bin with a lid and an automatic door, allowing users to easily dispose of used pads. The incineration process burns the pads efficiently while minimizing air pollution.
Alongside the incinerator, we are also developing a smart vending machine that provides easy access to sanitary pads in public spaces, schools, and workplaces. This solution ensures proper waste management and convenient product availability, promoting menstrual hygiene and sustainability in public and shared spaces.
